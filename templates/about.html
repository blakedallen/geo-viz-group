Our Project

Global warming is caused by excess greenhouse gases, such as C02. While this is a concerning fact, many of these numbers like total emissions donâ€™t really mean anything tangible to the average user. In this visualization we hope to simulate various global warming scenarios by overlaying sea level rise onto a visual map. Our intended audience is the general public, individuals who are concerned about climate change. The goal is to provide a visceral experience for the user to connect greenhouse gases to a real world effect such as losing land to sea level rise.

Disclaimer: The underlying model is a simplified linear model based on historical trend and does not reflect any climate science predictions.
